Bursar

Emeka Basil Anene

CGFM, CIA, CFE, CPA, ACA, MBA

V.P Financial Services/ Bursar

Emeka’s educational attainment began at Agulu Boys Grammar School, Agulu in Anambra State where he obtained his West African School Certificate (WASC) in 1971. He worked with Barclays Bank (now First Bank, PLC.) Ado Bayero Branch, Kano from 1972-1977 before proceeding to the United States for further studies.

He graduated from Western Kentucky University, Bowling Green, Kentucky with B.Sc in Accounting 1984, and MBA (Finance) from Mercer University Atlanta, Georgia in 1987.

He worked with the State of Georgia – Governor’s Office of Planning and Budget and the Georgia Department of Administrative Services Atlanta, Georgia, USA in various capacities as a professional accountant from (1993-2001). In 2001, he got employed by the Fulton County Government Atlanta, Georgia as an Internal Auditor and rose to Senior Internal Audit position from 2001 to 2011. At the end of May 2011 he came back home following his employment as the pioneer Vice-President Financial Services/Bursar of Adeleke University Ede, Osun State.

Emeka is a Nigerian Chartered Accountant (ACA), USA Certified Government Financial Manager (CGFM) with vast experience in financial planning and analysis, a Certified Internal Auditor (CIA) with wealth of skills and experience in identifying and developing effective internal control processes and procedures in public sectors, a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) and American Certified Public Accountant (CPA). He is affiliated with many International Accounting, Auditing and Financial professional bodies. He is married with five children.
