About Faculty of Science

The Faculty of Science (FOS) offer programs in basic training courses and fundamental sciences of Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics and Physics which form bedrock of all applied sciences and technology. In the Faculty programs, emphases are laid on developing the mind to acquire the knowledge and practical skills to explore, experiment, create and examine the world around. Starting from our immediate environment in pursuit of answers to probing questions of the society at large.

Admission requirement

Candidate must satisfy the University/Department requirement, i.e., five(5) credits at ordinary level or Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (SSCE), which must include Physics, Biology, Chemistry, English and Mathematics.

Coourses Offered

  • Biochemistry
  • Chemistry
  • Computer Science
  • Industrial Chemistry
  • Mathematics
  • Microbiology

Requirement for Graduation

  1. Students are to take a minimum of 16 credits and maximum of 21 credits per Semester
  2. Total requirement for graduation is 146 credits
  3. All the compulsory courses must be taken and passed
  4. programme/sub-discipline/Discipline structure to include period of formal Studies in the University, Industrial training, planned visit and projects
  5. A minimum of eight(8) ssemester and a maximum of twelve(12) semesters for a period of four(4) years is required
  6. A student accepted on transfer shall be obligated to spend at least three(3) sessions in the University before graduating.
  7. Under the Student Industrial Work Experience Scheme, Student shall be involved in six(6) months of Industrial work experience which shall form part of the four-year period of the study.
